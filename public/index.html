booKlub

booKlub is a social media for scholars, teachers, and book lovers in general.

With booKlub you can search for the book everyone's been talking about, add it to your bookShelf so others can see what you are reading, and even start your own booKlub. In these booKlubs, you can discuss your favorite aspects of the book, and talk about the deeper meanings of symbolism and literary elements you discover on your journey though it's pages.

booKlub connects a wide network of like-minded scholars, serving as a social media platform for everyone from authors to students, so feel to follow, share and connect with others.
